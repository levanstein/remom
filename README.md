# REM-MOM

**Your voice. Their comfort.**

Clone your voice to stay close to parents with dementia. Medication reminders, daily check-ins, and memory exercises... in the voice they know best.

## The Problem

55 million people worldwide live with dementia. The hardest part isn't forgetting. It's feeling forgotten. Familiar voices are encoded in deep procedural memory, the kind that dementia takes last. Your parent may not remember what day it is, but they remember the sound of your voice.

Existing medication reminder apps use cold, robotic voices. REM-MOM uses **yours**.

## How It Works

1. **Record your voice** (30 seconds)
2. **Choose a scenario** (medication reminder, morning check-in, memory exercise)
3. **Mom hears you** — your cloned voice, every day

## Tech Stack

- **ElevenLabs** Instant Voice Cloning + Text-to-Speech API
- **Node.js / Express** backend (API proxy)
- **Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS** frontend
- Deployed on **Replit**

## The Vision

- Real outbound phone calls to your parent's phone
- Memory journal tracking cognitive patterns over time
- Multi-family-member voice profiles (grandkids, siblings, friends)
- Caregiver dashboard with engagement and alertness metrics

The hardest part of dementia isn't forgetting.
It's feeling forgotten.

Your mom may not remember what day it is. But she remembers your voice. The sound of her child is encoded in the deepest layers of memory, the kind that dementia takes last.

Medication reminder apps use cold, robotic voices. Your mom doesn't need a robot. She needs you.
